MATPAPER respects intellectual property rights. If you are a copyright owner or authorized agent and believe your work has been posted without permission, you may submit a removal request below.

Our Policy

MATPAPER hosts fan-made and community artwork related to anime, manhwa, and gaming. We do not claim ownership of any third-party intellectual property.

Upon receiving a valid DMCA takedown notice, we will remove the infringing content promptly — typically within 48–72 hours.

What to Include in Your Request

  • Your full legal name and contact information
  • A description of the copyrighted work being infringed
  • The specific URL(s) of the infringing content on MATPAPER
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you are the copyright owner or authorized agent
  • Your electronic or physical signature

Counter-Notice

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may submit a counter-notice by contacting us with your identification, description of the removed content, and a statement under penalty of perjury that the removal was made in error.
