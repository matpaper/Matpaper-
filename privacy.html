Privacy Policy
Last updated: January 2026

Overview

MATPAPER ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

By using MATPAPER, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to improve your experience:

  • Local Storage Data: Your liked, saved, and recently viewed wallpapers are stored locally in your browser only — we never transmit this to our servers.
  • Usage Data: We track wallpaper views and downloads to improve content recommendations. This is anonymous and not linked to individuals.
  • Contact Form: If you contact us, we collect your name and email solely to respond to your message.
  • Cookies: We use cookies only to remember your preferences (e.g. cookie consent). We do not use advertising cookies unless AdSense is enabled.

How We Use Your Data

  • To display personalized content (recently viewed, saved wallpapers)
  • To track popularity of wallpapers (views and downloads)
  • To respond to your support or contact requests
  • To display relevant advertisements (if AdSense is enabled)

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Cloudinary — for image hosting and optimization
  • Appwrite — for database and authentication
  • Google AdSense — for advertising (if enabled)
  • Google Fonts — for typography

Data Retention & Deletion

Your locally stored preferences (liked/saved wallpapers) can be cleared at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or cache.

Contact us at our contact page if you wish to request deletion of any data we hold.

Children's Privacy

MATPAPER is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal data, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of MATPAPER after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, please use our contact page.
